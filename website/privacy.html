Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 12, 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

HueGrab is built with privacy as a core principle. We believe in transparency and your right to understand exactly how your data is (or isn't) used.

Data Collection

Simple answer: We don't collect any data.

HueGrab does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or usage data. This includes:

  • No user accounts or registration
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No crash reporting with personal data
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No usage statistics

Camera Access

HueGrab requires camera access to function. Here's exactly what happens:

  • The camera is used exclusively to capture images for color extraction
  • Images are processed locally on your device in real-time
  • No images are ever saved, stored, or transmitted anywhere
  • Once a color is extracted, the image data is immediately discarded
  • The app cannot access your photo library or previously taken photos

Data Storage

The only data stored on your device is:

  • Your camera permission preference (managed by your operating system)
  • No color history or capture history is saved

Third-Party Services

HueGrab does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app functions completely offline once installed.

Permissions

HueGrab only requests the following permission:

  • Camera: Required to capture images for color extraction

No other permissions are requested or required.

Children's Privacy

Since we don't collect any data, HueGrab is safe for users of all ages. The app is rated appropriate for everyone (4+ on iOS, Everyone on Android).

Open Source

HueGrab is open source. You can review the complete source code to verify our privacy claims: github.com/sam-red-26/HueGrab-app

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how HueGrab handles data, we will update this privacy policy and notify users through the app or website.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or HueGrab's privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: support@huegrab.com
GitHub: github.com/sam-red-26/HueGrab-app

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there's no data to request, modify, or delete. You maintain complete control over your device and the app at all times.

Summary

HueGrab is a simple, privacy-focused tool. We don't collect data because we don't need to. Your colors, your privacy, your device.
